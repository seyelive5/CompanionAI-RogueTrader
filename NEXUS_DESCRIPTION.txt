[size=4][b]Companion AI v2.2[/b][/size]

Provides role-based automatic combat decisions for party companions.

[size=3][b]Features[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Four strategies: Tank, DPS, Support, Balanced
[*]Timing-aware ability usage (buffs before attacks, finishers on low HP enemies)
[*]Ally-safe AoE checks
[*]Automatic reload management
[*]HP-cost ability safety checks
[/list]

[size=3][b]What This Mod Does[/b][/size]
When enabled, companions automatically select and use abilities based on their assigned role. The mod intercepts the game's AI decision-making and applies custom logic.

[size=3][b]What This Mod Does NOT Do[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Does not control the player character
[*]Does not control enemies or familiars/pets
[*]Does not guarantee optimal play in all situations
[/list]

[size=3][b]Strategies[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Tank[/b] - Defensive abilities, taunts, engages enemies
[*][b]DPS[/b] - Damage priority, uses finishers on low HP targets
[*][b]Support[/b] - Healing/buffs priority, maintains safe distance
[*][b]Balanced[/b] - Adapts to situation
[/list]

[size=3][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Unity Mod Manager 0.23.0+
[/list]

[size=3][b]Installation[/b][/size]
1. Install Unity Mod Manager
2. Extract mod files to: [code]%LOCALAPPDATA%Low\Owlcat Games\Warhammer 40000 Rogue Trader\UnityModManager\CompanionAI_v2.2\[/code]

[size=3][b]Configuration[/b][/size]
1. Press Ctrl+F10 in-game
2. Select CompanionAI v2.2 tab
3. Enable AI and select strategy for each companion

[size=3][b]Known Limitations[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Some ability combinations may not work as expected
[*]Unlisted abilities use fallback detection
[*]May conflict with other AI mods
[/list]

[size=3][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Tested with game version 1.2.x
[*]Compatible with ToyBox
[/list]
